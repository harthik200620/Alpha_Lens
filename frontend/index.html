Methodology. Levels are sized to each stock's ATR (Average True Range) — volatile names wider, quieter names tighter. We then manage the trade like a desk: book a partial profit at +1R and move the stop to breakeven, so a trade that runs in our favour then reverses still closes green instead of a full loss. Every outcome is netted of a round-trip transaction cost. A win = any trade that closes net-positive; we lead with expectancy & profit factor because at an asymmetric reward:risk a sub-50% win rate can still be highly profitable. No survivorship bias — every signal we publish, including time-stopped ones, is counted here.

How exits work: when price reaches 50% of the target (the +1R mark), half the quantity is booked and the stop moves to breakeven. A reversal after that exits the rest at breakeven — shown as 100%, a protected win, never a loss. Full target = the remaining half is booked; a stop hit = full exit.
